Frequently Asked Questions

Which cryptocurrencies can I use with the CryptoHub Card?

You can use over 20 cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Litecoin (LTC), Bitcoin Cash (BCH), Cardano (ADA), Solana (SOL), and many ERC-20 tokens. We're constantly adding support for more assets.

How are conversions handled at point of sale?

When you make a purchase, we instantly convert your cryptocurrency to the local currency at the current market rate. You can choose which crypto asset to spend from in the app before each transaction.

Is there a virtual card option?

Yes! Immediately after approval, you'll receive a virtual card that you can add to Apple Pay, Google Pay, or Samsung Pay. The physical card will arrive by mail within 7-10 business days.

How do I manage and track my spending?

The CryptoHub app provides real-time transaction notifications, detailed spending reports categorized by merchant type, and monthly statements. You can also set spending limits and freeze your card instantly.
