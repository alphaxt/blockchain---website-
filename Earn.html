Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about earning with CryptoHub

How does crypto staking work?
Staking involves participating in blockchain transaction validation. By locking your crypto, you help secure the network and earn rewards in return. The more you stake, the more rewards you earn.
Is staking safe?
Yes, staking on CryptoHub is secure. We use industry-leading security measures including cold storage for assets and multi-factor authentication. Your staked assets are insured against theft.
Can I unstake my assets at any time?
Most assets have flexible staking with no lock-up period. For higher APY options, we offer fixed terms from 30 days to 1 year. Early unstaking may incur a small fee.
How often are rewards paid?
Rewards are paid daily and compounded automatically. You can track your earnings in real-time through your CryptoHub dashboard.
Are there any fees for earning?
CryptoHub charges a small 5% fee on staking rewards. There are no fees for savings accounts or lending. All fees are clearly displayed before you commit.
