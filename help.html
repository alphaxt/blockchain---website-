Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a CryptoHub account?

To create a CryptoHub account:

  1. Visit our website and click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
  2. Enter your email address and create a strong password
  3. Verify your email address through the link we send you
  4. Complete identity verification (KYC process)
  5. Set up two-factor authentication for added security

The entire process takes about 10-15 minutes to complete.

How do I deposit funds into my CryptoHub wallet?

Depositing funds is easy:

  1. Log in to your CryptoHub account
  2. Navigate to your Wallet section
  3. Click "Deposit" and select the cryptocurrency you want to deposit
  4. Copy your unique deposit address or scan the QR code
  5. Send funds from your external wallet to this address

Note: Deposits may take some time to appear depending on blockchain confirmation times.

What security measures does CryptoHub implement?

At CryptoHub, security is our top priority. We implement:

  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) for all accounts
  • Bank-level encryption (AES-256)
  • Cold storage for 98% of user funds
  • Multi-signature wallets
  • Regular security audits
  • Withdrawal address whitelisting
  • Anti-phishing codes

We recommend enabling all available security features for maximum protection.

How long do withdrawals take to process?

Withdrawal processing times vary:

  • Cryptocurrency withdrawals: Typically processed within 5-30 minutes after approval. The actual blockchain confirmation time depends on network congestion.
  • Fiat currency withdrawals: Processed within 1-3 business days depending on your bank.

All withdrawals go through security verification which may take up to 24 hours for large amounts or new accounts.

What are the trading fees on CryptoHub?

Our fee structure is simple and competitive:

  • Maker fees: 0.10%
  • Taker fees: 0.15%

Fees decrease based on your 30-day trading volume:

  • ≥ $10,000: 0.09% maker / 0.14% taker
  • ≥ $50,000: 0.08% maker / 0.12% taker
  • ≥ $100,000: 0.06% maker / 0.10% taker

We also offer zero-fee stablecoin pairs and discounts for holding our native token.
